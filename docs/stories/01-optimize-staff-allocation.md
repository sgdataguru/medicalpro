# User Story: 1 - Optimize Staff Allocation with Predictive Analytics

**As a** hospital administrator,
**I want** to view predictive recommendations for optimal staff allocation across departments and shifts,
**so that** I can ensure adequate staffing levels, minimize overtime costs, and maintain quality of patient care.

## Acceptance Criteria

*   The system displays current staffing levels by department, role, and shift.
*   Predictive models recommend staffing adjustments based on historical patterns and forecasted patient volume.
*   Recommendations account for constraints such as staff availability, regulatory nurse-to-patient ratios, and budget limits.
*   The administrator can compare recommended allocations against current schedules.
*   Outputs include projected overtime costs and coverage gap warnings.

## Notes

*   Staffing is the first of five core modules identified for the platform.
*   Hershey emphasized that staffing changes cascade into other modules (e.g., bed allocation, revenue) — cross-module impact is covered in Story 11.
*   The sandbox will use synthetic staffing data for initial demos.
