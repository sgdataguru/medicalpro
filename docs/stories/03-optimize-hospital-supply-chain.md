# User Story: 3 - Optimize Hospital Supply Chain

**As a** hospital administrator,
**I want** to view supply chain optimization recommendations for medical supplies and equipment,
**so that** I can reduce waste, prevent stockouts, and control procurement costs.

## Acceptance Criteria

*   The system displays current inventory levels, consumption rates, and reorder points for key supplies.
*   Predictive models forecast supply demand based on patient volume trends and seasonal patterns.
*   Recommendations flag supplies at risk of expiration, overstocking, or stockout.
*   The administrator can compare current procurement patterns against optimized suggestions.
*   Cost savings projections are provided for recommended supply chain adjustments.

## Notes

*   Supply chain is one of the five core modules for the initial launch.
*   Hershey drew a parallel to her prior work with Campari (alcohol distribution) where warehouse shutdown simulations assessed supply chain impact — similar what-if scenarios apply here.
*   The simulation engine (Story 6) enables testing supply chain disruption scenarios.
