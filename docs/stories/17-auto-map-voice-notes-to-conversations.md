# User Story: 17 - Auto-map Voice Notes to Client Conversations

**As a** Relationship Manager,
**I want** my voice notes to be automatically mapped to the relevant client conversation records,
**so that** I can efficiently document interactions without manual data entry and maintain a complete client history.

## Acceptance Criteria

*   Voice notes recorded by the RM are transcribed and associated with the correct client record.
*   The system identifies the relevant client from the voice note content or context.
*   Transcribed notes are searchable within the client's conversation history.
*   The RM can review and correct the auto-mapping before finalizing.
*   Voice notes are stored securely and accessible from the client detail view.
