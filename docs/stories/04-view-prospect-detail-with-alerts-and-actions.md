# User Story: 4 - View Prospect Detail with Alerts and Suggested Actions

**As a** Relationship Manager,
**I want** to view a detailed prospect profile showing key metrics, alerts, and clear explanations of why the prospect is relevant,
**so that** I can have informed and timely conversations with clients around investments or asset movement.

## Acceptance Criteria

*   A detail panel (right-side) displays key metrics for the selected prospect.
*   Active alerts related to the prospect's liquidity events are shown prominently.
*   Clear explanations of why each prospect is relevant are provided.
*   Suggested next actions (e.g., "Initiate conversation," "Review portfolio") are displayed.
*   The detail view enables proactive, personal engagement rather than reactive follow-up.

## Notes

*   The purpose is not to replace relationships but to improve timing and provide better context for engagement.
*   Instead of reacting late, RMs should be able to reach out early to understand context.
