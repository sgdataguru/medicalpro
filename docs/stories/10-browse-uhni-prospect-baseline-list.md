# User Story: 10 - Browse Pre-defined UHNI Prospect Baseline List

**As a** Relationship Manager,
**I want** to browse a pre-defined baseline list of ultra high net worth individuals (100CR+),
**so that** I have a comprehensive starting point of potential prospects to target and track.

## Acceptance Criteria

*   The system contains a baseline list of approximately 4,400 UHNI individuals (100CR+ net worth).
*   Each entry includes available identifying information (name, associated companies, net worth range).
*   The list is searchable and filterable.
*   The list serves as the total addressable market (TAM) baseline for the platform.
*   All prospect cards/entries throughout the system reflect the 100CR+ threshold.

## Notes

*   The managing partner (Amit) stated this data is relatively easy to obtain and should be the baseline.
*   He referenced a specific number: "4,400 people have more than [100CR]."
*   For the initial demo/mockup, mock data will be used; real data sourcing will follow once the client agrees.
*   Some data providers have chargeable APIs — costs to be discussed with the client later.
