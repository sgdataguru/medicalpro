# User Story: 12 - Generate Prescriptive Action Recommendations

**As a** hospital administrator,
**I want** to receive prescriptive recommendations that suggest specific actions to optimize operations,
**so that** I can act on data-driven advice rather than just reviewing descriptive reports of what already happened.

## Acceptance Criteria

*   The system generates actionable recommendations based on predictive model outputs (e.g., "Reallocate 15 beds from Ward A to Ward B next month to reduce overflow risk by 30%").
*   Each recommendation includes the reasoning, supporting data, and expected outcome.
*   Recommendations are prioritized by potential impact and urgency.
*   The administrator can accept, defer, or dismiss recommendations with tracked rationale.
*   The system learns from accepted/dismissed recommendations to improve future suggestions over time.

## Notes

*   Hershey explicitly positioned the platform at the "predictive and prescriptive" level of analytics — beyond the "descriptive and diagnostic" that is standard in the market.
*   "The bottom line is so that they can make smart decisions based on fact, not based on assumption."
*   This is identified as a key market differentiator: "In Southeast Asia, I haven't seen anyone that is offering the advanced analytics, the descriptive, including simulation."
*   Prescriptive outputs are generated by the data science layer (algorithms, trends, and models) and surfaced through the intelligence engine.
