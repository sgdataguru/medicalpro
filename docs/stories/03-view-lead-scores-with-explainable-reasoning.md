# User Story: 3 - View Lead Scores with Explainable Reasoning

**As a** Relationship Manager,
**I want** to see a lead score for each prospect along with a clear explanation of the signals behind the score,
**so that** I can prioritize the right clients, decide when to engage, and understand why an action is recommended.

## Acceptance Criteria

*   Each prospect displays a lead score based on the strength and relevance of detected liquidity signals.
*   The score is accompanied by an explanation showing the reasoning and signals that contributed to it.
*   Prospects can be sorted by lead score to surface highest-priority opportunities.
*   The scoring logic is transparent and never presented as a black box.
*   The explanation includes specific data points (e.g., "IPO filing detected," "Promoter stake change identified").

## Notes

*   Lead scoring will always be explainable — this was emphasized as a key requirement.
*   The overall experience should remain simple, transparent, and actionable.
