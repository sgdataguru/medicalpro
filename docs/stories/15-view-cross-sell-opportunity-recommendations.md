# User Story: 15 - View Cross-sell Opportunity Recommendations

**As a** Relationship Manager,
**I want** to see cross-sell opportunity recommendations for my clients,
**so that** I can increase wallet share through relevant product suggestions aligned with client needs.

## Acceptance Criteria

*   The system identifies and displays cross-sell opportunities based on client profile and current product holdings.
*   Each recommendation includes the suggested product/service and the rationale behind it.
*   Recommendations are linked to the client's profile and liquidity events where applicable.
*   The RM can take action on a recommendation (e.g., mark as pursued, dismiss, schedule follow-up).
*   Opportunities are tracked across the portfolio for management visibility.

## Notes

*   "Cross-sell opportunity tracker" was listed as an additional element to incorporate.
*   This complements the wallet share analysis and lead scoring features.
