# User Story: 18 - View Client Retention and Attrition Metrics

**As a** Managing Partner,
**I want** to see client retention and attrition metrics on my dashboard,
**so that** I can identify at-risk clients early and direct the team to take preventive action before losing them.

## Acceptance Criteria

*   The dashboard displays client retention rate over configurable time periods.
*   Attrition metrics show the number and value of clients lost or at risk.
*   Churn risk scoring is applied to individual clients and visible in the overview.
*   Trends are visualized with historical comparisons (e.g., month-over-month, quarter-over-quarter).
*   At-risk clients can be drilled into for further investigation.

## Notes

*   "Client retention/attrition metrics dashboard" was listed as an additional element to incorporate.
*   "Conversion rates with churn risk scoring" was also listed as a Page 1 highlight.
*   This ties directly to the managing partner's concern about losing clients to competitors who act faster on liquidity events.
