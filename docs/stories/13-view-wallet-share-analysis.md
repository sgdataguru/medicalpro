# User Story: 13 - View Wallet Share Analysis for High-Value Clients

**As a** Managing Partner,
**I want** to see wallet share analysis for high-value clients,
**so that** I can identify untapped revenue potential and direct the team to deepen relationships with under-penetrated accounts.

## Acceptance Criteria

*   Wallet share is calculated and displayed for each high-value client.
*   The analysis shows the firm's current share of the client's total investable assets versus the estimated total.
*   Clients are ranked or highlighted by wallet share opportunity (lowest penetration = highest opportunity).
*   The data is presented with clear visualizations (e.g., bar chart, donut chart).
*   Filtering by RM or segment is supported.
