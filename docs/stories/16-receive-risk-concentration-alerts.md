# User Story: 16 - Receive Risk Concentration Alerts

**As a** Managing Partner,
**I want** to receive alerts when portfolio or regulatory risk concentrations exceed defined thresholds,
**so that** I can proactively manage exposure and ensure compliance before issues escalate.

## Acceptance Criteria

*   The system monitors portfolio-level risk concentrations (e.g., sector, geography, single client exposure).
*   Alerts are triggered when predefined thresholds are breached.
*   Each alert specifies the type of risk, the current concentration level, and the threshold breached.
*   Regulatory risk indicators are included where applicable.
*   Alerts are visible on the executive dashboard and in the notification system.

## Notes

*   "Risk concentration alerts (portfolio & regulatory)" was listed as an additional element to incorporate.
*   This supports the firm's compliance and risk management needs for UHNI client portfolios.
