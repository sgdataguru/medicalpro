# User Story: 4 - Analyze Hospital Revenue and Cost Drivers

**As a** hospital director,
**I want** to understand why revenue has changed year-over-year and identify where money is being burned,
**so that** I can make informed financial decisions and improve the hospital's financial health.

## Acceptance Criteria

*   The system displays revenue trends over configurable time periods (monthly, quarterly, yearly).
*   Cost breakdowns are shown by department, category (staffing, supplies, equipment, overhead), and trend direction.
*   The director can drill down from high-level revenue figures to specific cost drivers.
*   Variance analysis highlights significant deviations from expected financial performance.
*   The system surfaces the top contributing factors to revenue increases or decreases.

## Notes

*   Hershey described this module as "a little bit complex" since she is "not a finance girl" — the exact scope of finance analytics is still being refined.
*   A specific use case mentioned: "Why is my revenue this year lower than last year? Where am I burning more money?"
*   This module connects to staffing and bed allocation through the simulation engine (Story 6) to model financial impact of operational decisions.
