# User Story: 2 - Optimize Strategic Bed Allocation

**As a** hospital administrator,
**I want** to receive data-driven recommendations for strategic bed allocation across departments,
**so that** I can maximize hospital revenue, reduce patient wait times, and improve throughput.

## Acceptance Criteria

*   The system displays current bed occupancy rates by department and ward.
*   Predictive models forecast bed demand based on admission trends, seasonal patterns, and scheduled procedures.
*   Recommendations suggest reallocation of beds across departments to optimize utilization.
*   The administrator can see projected revenue impact of allocation changes.
*   Alerts are raised when occupancy is projected to exceed capacity or drop below efficient thresholds.

## Notes

*   Bed allocation was specifically called out as "the money maker" for hospitals.
*   Singapore General Hospital (SGH) was referenced as an example with 4,000 beds.
*   This module is tightly linked to staffing (Story 1) and finance (Story 4) via the simulation engine (Story 6).
