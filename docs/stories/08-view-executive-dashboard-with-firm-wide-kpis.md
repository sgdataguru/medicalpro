# User Story: 8 - View Executive Dashboard with Firm-wide KPIs

**As a** Managing Partner,
**I want** to see a high-level executive dashboard displaying firm-wide AUM, growth rates, attrition, and key liquidity event signals,
**so that** I can monitor overall business health at a glance and ensure the team is capturing opportunities on time.

## Acceptance Criteria

*   The dashboard (Page 1) shows firm-wide AUM with growth rates.
*   Client attrition metrics are displayed.
*   Liquidity event signals with 30–90 day triggers are summarized.
*   Revenue growth tracking linked to product mix is visible.
*   Alternate penetration percentage across client segments is shown.
*   Credit/capital solutions visibility is included.
*   Conversion rates with churn risk scoring are presented.
*   The layout is clean, uncluttered, and uses summary graphs to convey key information.
*   All prospect data reflects the 100CR+ UHNI baseline.

## Notes

*   The managing partner emphasized wanting to see this first page with less clutter and key summary numbers.
*   This page is the entry point — clicking on items drills down to the detailed RM-level view (Page 2).
*   The total addressable market is approximately 4,000–4,400 individuals with 100CR+ net worth.
*   There are approximately 30–35 RMs in the organization.
