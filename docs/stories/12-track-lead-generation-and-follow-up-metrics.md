# User Story: 12 - Track Lead Generation and RM Follow-up Metrics

**As a** Managing Partner,
**I want** to see a dashboard tracking how many leads and liquidity events are generated and how many are followed up by Relationship Managers,
**so that** I can measure team effectiveness and ensure opportunities are not being missed.

## Acceptance Criteria

*   The dashboard shows the total number of leads generated over a configurable time period.
*   The number of liquidity events detected is displayed alongside leads generated.
*   Follow-up status for each lead is tracked (e.g., contacted, in conversation, converted, not actioned).
*   Conversion rates from lead to engagement are visible.
*   Data can be filtered by RM, time period, or event type.

## Notes

*   The transcript mentions: "A small dashboard can track how many leads and liquidity events are generated and followed up by Relationship Managers."
*   This ties into the managing partner's concern about not reaching liquidity events on time.
